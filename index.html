Telecommunications

Micro Interactions

SmartBar

Horizontal bar component on top of the bottom navigation to support the visibility of other card after first fold screen.

Interaction:

  • Rolling Text to show more information
  • The SmartBar is linked to specific SmartCard, tapping on SmartBar will bring user to that SmartCard.
  • When user scrolling down and passing through the linked SmartCard, SmartBar will transform to be the SmartCard and dissapear.

Screen Recording:

  • https://drive.google.com/file/d/1rx6V-pzj1my0zlkD0VtRGX3S54a0Xsu1/view?usp=sharing

Product Catalogue

List of available internet packages from Smartfren.

Interaction:

  • Collapsible Header when scrolling up through the list of internet packages.
  • Category Product will be the title of the screen and Search Bar will resize.
  • Tapping on subcategory will automatically scroll the list to the subcategory.
  • Scrolling through subcategory in the list will automatically change the selected subcategory on the top.
  • Side Menu to display category and subcategory, select subcategory here will automatically bring user to that subcategory.

Screen Recording:

  • https://drive.google.com/file/d/1kqsewUBcKeqDXqk165qZx03UmyDcdS0I/view?usp=sharing
